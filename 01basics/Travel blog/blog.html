Scouting New York As a movie location scout in New York City, Nick Carr puts miles in each week in search of the perfect setting and chronicles his finds on his blog. He’s toured countless spots throughout the five boroughs, from luxury apartments to rooftops to subway tunnels. As an urban explorer, he’s discovered places that even lifelong New Yorkers don’t know about, and claims that “every street has a hidden gem or two if you take the time to look.” So the next time you stroll down the block where you live, stare like a scout because you, too, might unearth a New York treasure that’s longing to be found.
